<h3 align="center">
The missing piece between your app and the TV remote.
</h3>

- **Automatic spatial algorithm**: No manual "on right press, focus element X" wiring.
  Point your components in a direction and the library figures out what to focus next.
- **Built for TV, works everywhere**: Actively used in production on Tizen, webOS,
  Hisense, Vizio, and Chromium-based STBs. Not an afterthought port.
- **Light and low-dependency**: Simple API, minimal footprint, no runtime surprises
  on constrained TV hardware.
